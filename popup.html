Ownership
Show your own thinking — what you've tried, your current understanding, or constraints you're working within.
"I've tried X but it fails because…"
"My constraint is that I can't use Y."
"I think the issue is Z, but I'm not sure."
Depth
Ask why and how, not just what. Prompts that seek understanding score higher than those that just request an output.
"Why does this approach work better than…?"
"How does X relate to Y under the hood?"
"What are the underlying principles here?"
Critical
Probe edge cases, tradeoffs, and alternatives. Ask the AI to challenge its own answer or consider failure modes.
"What are the risks or downsides of this?"
"What would break this approach?"
"What alternatives should I consider?"
Clarity
Be specific and well-contextualized. Name your tools, audience, format, and goal so the AI has no room to guess.
"I'm using Python 3.11 with FastAPI."
"Format the response as bullet points."
"The audience is a non-technical manager."
